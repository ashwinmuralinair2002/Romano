About Me

Ciao! I'm Fabrizio Romano, a football journalist born in Naples, Italy, on February 21, 1993. I’ve always been passionate about the beautiful game, and I turned that passion into a career in football transfer reporting.

My journey started in 2009, and things really took off when I broke the news about Mauro Icardi's move from Barcelona. Since then, I’ve worked with Sky Sport Italy, The Guardian, and CBS Sports, sharing reliable transfer news with fans across the world.

You might know me for my catchphrase — "Here we go!" — which I use to confirm transfer deals. It’s become a global signal that something big is happening in football.

I’m grateful to have been recognized with awards like Best Football Journalist at the Globe Soccer Awards, and I was even named in Forbes’ 30 Under 30 in media and marketing. I speak Italian, English, Spanish, and Portuguese to stay connected with sources and fans around the world.

Outside of journalism, I'm also a big fan of Watford FC. Thank you for following my work and joining me on this amazing football journey.
